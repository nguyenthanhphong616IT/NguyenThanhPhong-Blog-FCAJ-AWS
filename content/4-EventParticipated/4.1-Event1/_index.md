# FCAJ Community Day 09-05

### Purpose of the Event

- How to have a proactive learning mindset, leverage resources, and not be afraid to ask questions.
- Introducing a learning method to create Documents using Proptimizer and create prompts for AI.
- Introducing AI services and AI tools to support the development lifecycle.
- Skills to prepare in the AI era.

### List of Speakers
- **Huynh Hoang Long** - FCAJ Mentor from AWS Vietnam.
- **Nguyen Tuan Thinh** - FCAJ Mentor from AWS Vietnam.
- **Anh Khang** - Solutions Architect at Cloud Kinetics Vietnam.
- **Ms. Thao** - Software Developer at VIB International Bank.

### Highlights

### 1. Speaker Huynh Hoang Long – FCAJ Mentor from AWS Vietnam

**Topic:** *Addicted to Learning Like You're Addicted to Social Media*

Mr. Huynh Hoang Long shared about a proactive learning mindset in the technology era, especially how to turn learning into a natural habit similar to how people use social media daily. The main content emphasized not learning passively or trying to solve problems alone for too long when encountering issues.

An important point in his sharing is the method: **"If you don't know for 2 minutes, ask immediately"**. When facing an error, not understanding a document, or not knowing how to deploy, learners should proactively ask mentors, the community, or use support tools like AI to save time and learn faster.

**Results or values achieved:**
Through this presentation, I learned the lesson of a proactive learning spirit, not being afraid to ask, and not getting stuck on a problem for too long. The content helped me improve my self-learning skills, questioning skills, and how to utilize the community and support tools to enhance learning efficiency during internships and projects.

---

### 2. Speaker Nguyen Tuan Thinh – FCAJ Mentor from AWS Vietnam

**Topic:** *Automated Prompt Engineering: Enhancing LLM Output Quality*

Mr. Nguyen Tuan Thinh presented how to optimize commands when working with large language models like LLMs. The content focused on creating effective prompts to help AI better understand requirements, thereby generating accurate, complete results tailored to the usage goal.

Besides, he also mentioned the topic **Token Economics: Understanding AI Costs**, helping participants understand that when using AI, every prompt and response involves token costs. Therefore, writing clear, concise, and focused prompts not only improves output quality but also optimizes AI usage costs.

Mr. Thinh also introduced **Proptimizer AI**, a Chrome extension that supports prompt optimization:
https://d3jqm7so635aqb.cloudfront.net/optimizer

**Results or values achieved:**
From this sharing, I learned how to write clearer prompts, optimize commands to get better results from AI, and understand more about token costs when using LLM tools. This knowledge can be directly applied to learning, researching documents, writing reports, analyzing code errors, and supporting project development.

---

### 3. Speaker Anh Khang – Solutions Architect at Cloud Kinetics

**Topic:** *AI-Ready Freshers: Skills and Mindset in the AI Era*

Mr. Khang shared about the necessary skills and mindset for students, freshers, or beginners starting their careers in the AI era. The content emphasized that newcomers need not only professional knowledge but also adaptability, a continuous learning mindset, and the ability to use AI as a work support tool.

The presentation helped participants better understand the actual requirements of businesses for freshers in the technology field. Besides technical skills, soft skills such as communication, self-learning, problem-solving, teamwork, and critical thinking are also very important.

**Results or values achieved:**
Through this sharing, I better understood the skills needed to prepare for working in a modern technology environment. I realized that AI does not completely replace humans but is a tool to improve performance if used correctly. This content helped me better orient my training in professional skills, soft skills, and professional mindset.

---

### 4. Speaker Ms. Thao – Software Developer at VIB International Bank

**Topic:** *BMAD METHOD – Bringing Agile and AI to Your IDE*

Ms. Thao introduced the **BMAD Method**, a method combining Agile mindset and AI to support the software development process right within the IDE environment. The content focused on how to use AI not only for simple Q&A but also to support requirement analysis, planning, coding, debugging, and improving project quality.

She also shared how to apply the BMAD Method in practice, especially in open-source projects or personal projects. This method helps learners know how to organize work more clearly, break down requirements, coordinate roles in the software development process, and leverage AI to increase work efficiency.

**Results or values achieved:**
From this presentation, I learned how to systematically apply AI to the software project process. The content helped me understand more about the Agile mindset, how to break down tasks, manage requirements, and use AI as an assistant during coding. This is knowledge that can be directly applied to study projects, group projects, or internship projects.
